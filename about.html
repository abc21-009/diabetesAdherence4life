About DiabetesAdhere4Life

Committed to improving health outcomes through medication adherence and support

Our Mission

At DiabetesAdhere4Life, we as University of Botswana final year Student Nurses are dedicated to empower individuals living with diabetes to take control of their health through proper medication adherence and lifestyle management.

Our initiative was launched in 2025 after we recognized a critical gap in diabetes care: despite advances in medication and technology, many patients continue to struggle with consistently following their prescribed treatment plans.

We believe that with the right support, education, and tools, everyone with diabetes can achieve better health outcomes and live a fuller, more active life.

Our Approach

We take a holistic, patient-centered approach to medication adherence that recognizes the complexity of managing a chronic condition like diabetes.

  • Education: We provide clear, accessible information about diabetes medications, their benefits, and how they work.
  • Practical Tools: From medication trackers to reminder systems, we offer resources that make adherence easier to maintain.
  • Community Support: We connect patients with peers who understand the challenges of diabetes management.
  • Professional Guidance: Our network of healthcare experts provides evidence-based recommendations and support.

Our Core Values

These principles guide everything we do at DiabetesAdhere4Life.

Patient-Centered Care

We put patients first, recognizing that each person's experience with diabetes is unique and deserving of individualized support.

Evidence-Based Approach

Our recommendations and resources are grounded in the latest scientific research and clinical best practices.

Accessibility

We are committed to making diabetes care information and resources available to all, regardless of background or circumstances.

Innovation

We continuously seek new and better ways to support medication adherence through technology and creative solutions.
